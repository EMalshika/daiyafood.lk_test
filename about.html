We are Daiya Food

At the outset, it was just a dream, but now it has transformed into a delightful and authentic taste of homemade cuisine available online. What sets our offerings apart is our infusion of the fiery flavors of Sri Lankan cuisine. Our commitment to your well-being means we prepare our dishes in traditional clay pots, ensuring that your meals are not only delicious but also healthy.

Furthermore, we take pride in our dedication to sustainability, using locally sourced ingredients and eco-friendly packaging to minimize our environmental footprint. Additionally, our experienced chefs continually innovate to bring you exciting and diverse menu options that cater to your culinary cravings.
